My journey as a newbie in tech hasn't been easy but the joy that comes from playing with colors when designing website is a source of motivation to do better every day, while i had a little problem understanding intermediate CSS(cascading styling sheet) so i decide to build this mini project so i could put to practice what have learnt so far on HTML and CSS and along side the knowlegde i got from an article i read recently on the psychology of color in web design, it talks about how powerful the choice of color we use when designing a webite is and how it strengthen the brands. it also talks about what particular color to use when designing a website for various organisation, as most designers are not good at choosing colors
  • Blue color is used mostly for social networking app and tech brands e.g facebook and intel
  • Red color is perfect for food e.g coca cola and KFC
  • Green color is awesome for nature and wildlife e.g NAT GEO wild
  • Purple color for beauty product and feminine brands e.g lovellberry
  • White color is associated with science and health related sites e.g VEDI medical clinic
It is important that a web designer understand the concepts of colors,how and when to use them when designing a website.

This project is about four ways or designs that colors palettes or colour schemes can be display to help designers choose various shades of colors to use when designing a website, the 'A's contains monochromatic color of a single hue and are changed by extending it Tones (darker shades) and Tints (lighter shades). the 'B's are multicolored containing different colors. i represent each color by their names instead of their HEX codes or RGB values because most designers are carried away by HEX codes and RGB values that they forget the name of colors they use everyday,no matter the shades of a color they still have a name they are called though they might not be among the 147 predefined color that are recognized by browsers.
